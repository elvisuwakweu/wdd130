Ebonyi State

Ebonyi State, created in 1996 with Abakaliki as its capital, is in southeastern Nigeria. Known as the "Salt of the Nation" for its salt lakes, it is mainly an agricultural state producing crops like rice, yam, and cassava. Ebonyi has beautiful natural attractions, vibrant Igbo culture, and growing infrastructure, making it an emerging center for tourism and business.
